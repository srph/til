title: Chrome Desktop Notifications
---

It's easy as:

```js
Notification.requestPermission();
new Notification('Hello, { body: 'This is awesome' });
```

Here's a [demo](http://codepen.io/srph/pen/mJmXMw) where I added `Notification` to one of my pens.
